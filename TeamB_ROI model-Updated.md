# ROI Model — Digital Grocery Initiative

> **Scope:** Developed on the example of **Nikora Store**, but scalable to other Georgian grocery chains.

---

##  Investment

| Component | Estimated Cost (GEL) |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Technology + Implementation | 3.6M – 8.4M |

---

##  Revenue Impact

**Projected uplift: +2% to +5% additional sales within 3 years**

Driven by:
- Increased purchase frequency
- Convenience-driven demand capture
- Improved customer retention

---

## Cost Impact

**Projected efficiency improvement: 1% – 3%**

Through:
- Better inventory management
- Reduced waste
- Improved targeting and personalization

---

##  Payback Period

| Scenario | Payback Period |
|----------|----------------|
|  Upside | 2.5 – 3.5 years |
| Base Case | 4 – 5 years |
| Downside | 6 – 8 years |

---

## Key Value Driver

> **Customer adoption rate of online grocery**

The speed and depth of consumer adoption is the single most critical variable determining whether returns land in the upside or downside scenario.

---

##  Scalability Note

This model was built using **Nikora Store** as the reference case, but the framework and assumptions are designed to be portable across other Georgian grocery chains with adjustments for:
- Store network size and density
- Existing digital infrastructure
- Customer base demographics
- Regional market dynamics
