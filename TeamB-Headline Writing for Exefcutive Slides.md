# Executive Headlines — PSP Healthcare Ecosystem Strategy

## Industry and Competitive Context
Georgian pharmacy retail is structurally fragmented: no player owns the healthcare-to-purchase journey, creating a clear opportunity for platform integration

## AI Landscape
AI enables personalization and automation across the value chain, with proven potential to increase revenue by 5–15% and reduce costs by up to 30%

## Platform Dynamics
Platform models create strong network effects by connecting doctors, influencers, and customers, making early movers significantly more defensible over time

## Strategic Recommendation
Option 3 — Healthcare Ecosystem Platform — is the only strategy that captures the full value chain and resolves market fragmentation

## Transformation Roadmap
A phased approach starting with data integration and personalization (Option 2) reduces execution risk while enabling a scalable platform build

## Financial Case
Base case delivers positive cash flow by Year 3 and achieves payback by Year 5, with significant upside driven by platform adoption and network effects
