# Three Preliminary Strategic Options

> **Format:** One sentence + rationale per option.

---

## Option 1 — Build a Full In-House Online Grocery Platform 

**Strategy:** Develop and own a proprietary digital grocery platform end-to-end.

**Rationale:** Provides full control over customer data, pricing, and experience, enabling long-term competitive advantage in an omnichannel market.

---

## Option 2 — Partner with Delivery Platforms (Wolt / Glovo-First Strategy)

**Strategy:** Enter the online grocery market by distributing through existing third-party delivery platforms.

**Rationale:** Enables fast market entry with lower upfront investment, but sacrifices margin and customer ownership.

---

## Option 3 — Hybrid Model (Own Platform + Delivery Partnerships)

**Strategy:** Launch via delivery partnerships for speed while simultaneously building a proprietary platform.

**Rationale:** Balances speed and control by leveraging existing delivery infrastructure while building long-term digital capabilities.
