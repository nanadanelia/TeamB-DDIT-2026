# Georgian Retail Industry's Digital Ecosystem Transformation (Phase 1)

## Core Problem
Cross-border e-commerce is growing at **56.7% YoY**, nearly **8x faster** than the offline Georgian market.  
Local incumbents risk losing the **"Data Layer"** to delivery aggregators (Wolt/Glovo).

---

## Governing Thought
We must transition the Georgian grocery sector from a **"Physical Density"** model to a **"Logistics & Data Velocity"** model.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Stop Value Export**  
   Retain the GEL 1.4bn currently leaving the Georgian ecosystem for foreign platforms.

2. **Efficiency Uplift**  
   Achieve the **3.4x productivity gain** (Wharton Benchmark) in procurement and inventory by implementing AI-driven forecasting.

3. **Basket Expansion**  
   Lift the average digital transaction from **GEL 16 → GEL 25+** via AI-powered **"Smart Replenishment"** bundles.

---

## Success Metric
Achieve measurable business impact within the **18-month ROI window**.
