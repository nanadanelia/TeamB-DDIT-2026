# AI Use Disclosure

## Purpose
This document discloses the use of AI tools in the preparation of project materials and reports, ensuring transparency and academic integrity.

---

## AI Tools Used
- **ChatGPT (OpenAI)** – Used to assist in structuring, organizing, and drafting textual content, including analysis summaries, strategic frameworks, and documentation.

---

## Scope of AI Assistance
AI tools were used **only to draft, organize, and format information**.  
All **interpretations, conclusions, and final decisions** are made by the author.

---

## Statement of Responsibility
The author confirms full responsibility for:

- Accuracy and validity of all content
- Final analysis, conclusions, and recommendations
- Compliance with academic and organizational integrity standards

---

## Suggested Usage
This disclosure can be included in:

- Appendices of academic reports
- Prefaces or introductions of projects
- Documentation of professional or research work

---

> Example phrasing for reports:
> "Some sections of this report were drafted using AI-assisted writing tools (ChatGPT). All final interpretations, conclusions, and recommendations were authored and verified by the project lead."
