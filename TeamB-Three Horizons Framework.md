# Three Horizons Framework — PSP Pharmaceutical Platform

## Source
McKinsey strategy research. Applied to AI-driven transformation in pharmaceutical retail.

---

## Core idea

PSP must manage three portfolios in parallel:

- H1: Immediate AI-driven efficiency and revenue gains (Deploy Now + Optimize)
- H2: Build data, infrastructure, and scalable AI capabilities (Invest to Enable)
- H3: Develop future AI-driven healthcare platform models (Experiment)

---

## The three horizons

| Horizon | Time frame | Focus | AI-era characteristics |
|---|---|---|---|
| **H1: Defend and Extend** | Now – 12 months | Optimize current pharmacy operations | AI improves efficiency, reduces costs, increases sales |
| **H2: Nurture and Build** | 12 – 36 months | Build data and platform capabilities | AI becomes embedded into core systems |
| **H3: Create and Options** | 36+ months | Create new healthcare platform models | AI enables new business models and ecosystems |

---

## Horizon 1 — Defend and Extend (Deploy Now + Optimize)

| Initiative | Investment | Funding source |
|---|---|---|
| Demand Forecasting AI | Low–Medium | Existing operations |
| AI Customer Service Agents | Low | Cost savings (support) |
| AI Workforce Scheduling | Low | Operational budget |
| AI Marketing Content Generation | Low | Marketing budget |

**Logic:**  
- High readiness, immediate ROI  
- Improves:
  - availability (forecasting)  
  - cost efficiency (support, workforce)  
  - conversion (marketing)  

👉 Generates **cash + credibility**

---

## Horizon 2 — Nurture and Build (Invest to Enable)

| Initiative | Investment | Funding source |
|---|---|---|
| Dynamic Pricing (OTC & Skincare) | Medium | H1 savings |
| Dynamic Inventory Allocation | Medium–High | H1 reinvestment |

**Logic:**  
- Requires:
  - integrated data across stores + online  
  - pricing governance  
  - system-level decision-making  

👉 Moves PSP from **AI tools → AI-driven operations**

---

## Horizon 3 — Create and Options (Experiment)

| Initiative | Investment | Funding source |
|---|---|---|
| Conversational Health Assistant | Medium–High | H2 platform |
| Automated Replenishment System | Medium–High | H2 infrastructure |

**Logic:**  
- AI starts acting as:
  - advisor (health assistant)  
  - decision-maker (replenishment)  

👉 Early step toward:
- personalized healthcare journeys  
- semi-autonomous pharmacy operations  

---

## Sequencing logic

| Horizon | Prerequisite | Consequence if skipped |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | Clean operational data + basic governance | AI remains isolated pilots |
| H2 | Integrated data across channels | Pricing and inventory cannot scale |
| H3 | Strong data foundation + system integration | AI cannot act autonomously |

---

## Funding logic

- H1 → generates **efficiency gains + quick wins**  
- H2 → funded by H1 savings  
- H3 → funded after infrastructure is ready  

---

## Strategic narrative (what you say in class)

PSP should:

1. **Win now** by improving efficiency and customer experience using AI  
2. **Build capabilities** by integrating data and enabling smarter decisions  
3. **Transform** into a more intelligent, semi-autonomous healthcare system  

---

## Key takeaway

This is not three separate strategies:

- H1 creates **cash and trust**
- H2 builds **capability**
- H3 creates **future advantage**

Without H2, H3 is not executable.
