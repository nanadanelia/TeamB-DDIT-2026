## Horizon Definitions

### Horizon 1 — Months 1–6
Deployable with PSP's current capabilities. Focuses on foundation-building, MVP launch, platform launch campaign, partnership activation, content engine, loyalty pilot, change management, and data governance. Does not rely on immediate revenue; value comes from engagement, loyalty activation, and data accumulation.

### Horizon 2 — Months 7–18
Requires additional investment before value is fully visible. Focuses on AI recommendation engine, advisor appointment service, advanced personalization, sponsored campaigns, and ecosystem performance dashboard. Approved if H1 shows measurable platform usage, loyalty engagement, and early recommendation-driven discovery.

### Horizon 3 — Post-18 months
High-potential option space. Not committed spend. Activated only if H1 and H2 show sufficient adoption, data readiness, engagement, and measurable contribution to retention or skincare and wellness sales growth.

---

## Dependency Logic

Data governance (01) is the single most critical prerequisite — no AI initiative can deploy without clean customer data, consent logic, and defined data ownership. MVP platform (02) must be live before loyalty pilot (07) and advisor service (09). Partnership activation (05) must precede content engine (06). AI engine (08) must be live before segmentation (10) and sponsored campaigns (11). No H2 initiative begins before Month 7. No H3 initiative is committed until H2 adoption is independently verified against agreed KPI thresholds.

---

## Funding Narrative

### H1
Horizon 1 does not rely on immediate direct revenue. Its financial contribution comes from early customer engagement, loyalty activation, increased skincare and wellness product interaction, and improved digital participation. These signals validate whether the ecosystem can influence repeat purchasing, retention, and customer lifetime value.

### H2
Horizon 2 requires an additional GEL 65,000 committed investment, approved if H1 shows measurable platform usage, loyalty engagement, creator participation, and early evidence of recommendation-driven product discovery.

### H3
Horizon 3 remains an option space rather than an immediate commitment. PSP should move H3 initiatives from option to commitment only if the platform reaches agreed adoption thresholds, recommendation conversion improves, creator and advisor participation remains active, and the ecosystem demonstrates measurable contribution to retention or skincare and wellness sales growth.
