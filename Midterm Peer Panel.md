# Midterm Peer Panel — Challenge Questions for Prediction Market Presentation

## 1. ROI / Financial Rigor Challenge

Your upside scenario assumes **10,000 monthly active traders** with **GEL 700 monthly trading volume per user** by Year 1.

Given that Georgian retail participation in capital markets is currently limited, what evidence supports this adoption assumption?

Also, what happens to platform viability if trading activity is **50% lower** than projected?

## 2. Implementation Challenge

Your roadmap places liquidity support after regulatory and data governance preparation.

What specifically creates enough early market activity and liquidity for users to trust the platform before network effects develop?

## 3. Recommendation Challenge

You recommend the **TBC-backed pathway** because of trust and distribution advantages.

What specific external condition would make the **crypto-native VASP option** strategically preferable instead?

The strongest challenge is the **ROI / Financial Rigor Challenge**, because their model depends heavily on adoption, liquidity, and trading volume assumptions.
