## 3. Priority Use Cases

### Deploy Now
1. **Demand forecasting**  
Improves product availability, reduces stockouts and overstock, and directly impacts revenue and working capital.

2. **AI-powered customer service agents**  
Automates routine inquiries (availability, usage, delivery), reducing cost and improving customer experience.

---

### Invest to Enable
1. **Dynamic pricing (OTC & skincare)**  
Improves margins and competitiveness but requires strong data integration and pricing governance.

2. **Dynamic inventory allocation**  
Ensures optimal stock distribution across stores and online channels, increasing sell-through and reducing waste.

---

## 4. Key Insight

In pharmaceutical retail, the strongest AI opportunities are in:
- **Prediction (forecasting, recommendations)** → immediate ROI  
- **Customer interaction (AI agents)** → cost + experience improvement  

More advanced opportunities depend on:
- integrated data  
- platform infrastructure  
- regulatory and trust considerations  

---

## 5. Data Advantage

PSP’s advantage comes from:
- transaction data  
- prescription and OTC purchase behavior  
- customer health and skincare preferences  

Over time, this enables:
- better personalization  
- stronger recommendations  
- higher customer lifetime value
