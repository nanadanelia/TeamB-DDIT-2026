## 3. Priority Use Cases

### Deploy Now
1. **Demand forecasting**  
AI improves demand predictions, which reduces stockouts and excess inventory. This creates value by lowering working capital needs and protecting revenue through better product availability.

2. **AI-powered customer service agents**  
AI handles large volumes of routine customer interactions automatically, reducing support costs and improving response times. This creates value through labor efficiency, better service availability, and a more scalable customer support model.

### Invest to Enable
1. **Autonomous warehouse operations**  
This has major long-term cost and efficiency potential, but it requires physical infrastructure changes, automation systems, and significant investment before value can be captured.

2. **Dynamic pricing optimization**  
This can materially improve margins, but it depends on real-time data integration, pricing governance, and stronger analytical infrastructure than many retailers currently have.

---

## 4. Business Case for Two Deploy Now Use Cases

### Demand Forecasting
AI creates value by improving inventory decisions before products are purchased and distributed. For a large retailer, even a 10% reduction in excess inventory or stockout-related losses could translate into a meaningful improvement in margin and working capital.

### AI-Powered Customer Service Agents
AI creates value by automating repetitive service interactions such as order tracking, return questions, and basic support requests. If a retailer shifts a large share of routine inquiries away from human agents, the financial impact could include double-digit reductions in customer service cost alongside better response speed.

---

## 5. Key Insight
The strongest AI opportunities in retail are concentrated in activities where better prediction or lower-cost interaction directly improves an important business metric. In the short term, the biggest wins come from forecasting, recommendations, and customer service, while more transformative opportunities depend on better infrastructure and data integration.

---

## 6. Data Advantage
Retailers with strong customer, transaction, and operational data are better positioned to benefit from AI because better data supports better predictions. Over time, this can create a reinforcing advantage in personalization, forecasting, and decision quality.
