| Cumulative NPV | (979,902) | 2,305,926 | 7,358,654 | 14,002,878 | 21,782,766 |

**NPV formula:** PV = CF / (1 + r)^n where r = 10%, n = year number
**IRR formula:** Solve for r where NPV = 0: −1,078,000 + 3,978,000/(1+r) + 6,728,000/(1+r)² + 9,728,000/(1+r)³ + 12,528,000/(1+r)⁴ = 0
**Payback formula:** Year 1 deficit GEL 1,078,000 ÷ Year 2 monthly cash flow (GEL 3,978,000 ÷ 12 = GEL 331,500/month) = 3.25 months into Year 2 = ~16 months total
