# 6. Base Case Financials

PSP already operates at significant scale within the Georgian pharmacy and healthcare market.

According to publicly available financial reporting:

- PSP generated approximately GEL 634 million revenue in 2020
- operating profit reached approximately GEL 45 million
- advertising expenses exceeded GEL 7.5 million annually

In 2021:
- retail pharmaceutical revenue exceeded GEL 512 million
- wholesale pharmaceutical revenue exceeded GEL 164 million
- medical services revenue exceeded GEL 64 million
- net profit increased to approximately GEL 61.8 million

These figures are strategically important because the proposed ecosystem investment remains relatively small compared to PSP’s existing operational scale.

The proposed ecosystem is therefore modeled not as a standalone business requiring independent scale creation, but as a platform enhancement layer designed to improve monetization of existing customer flows through:

- stronger loyalty engagement
- increased repeat purchases
- personalized recommendations
- skincare and wellness ecosystem participation
- higher customer lifetime value
- creator and medical professional partnerships

# Strategic Financial Interpretation

The proposed ecosystem is not expected to generate immediate direct revenue after launch.

Instead, the financial logic is based on gradual behavioral improvements across PSP’s existing customer base, including:
- stronger ecosystem engagement
- improved personalization
- higher skincare and wellness participation
- repeat purchasing behavior
- stronger loyalty retention
- increased customer lifetime value

As a result, the ecosystem should primarily be evaluated as a long-term strategic customer engagement investment rather than a short-term transactional e-commerce project.
